Research Projects & Grants

Our research group is supported by various national and regional funding agencies. Below are the current and past research projects led by our team members.

Current Projects

National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC)

Blazar分类和辐射机制

This project aims to investigate the physical mechanisms behind the extreme variability observed in blazars by combining multi-wavelength data from radio to gamma-ray bands. We focus on understanding particle acceleration processes, magnetic field structures, and the disk-jet connection in active galactic nuclei.

Keywords: Blazars, Multi-wavelength astrophysics, Jet physics, Time-domain astronomy
